Recommendations
1. Cash-flow–aware nudges: Predict low-balance windows and send timely alerts or offer short-term liquidity (reduces overdrafts and churn).
2. Behavioral segmentation: Cluster customers by spend/volatility to personalize offers and risk controls.
3. Real-time anomaly detection: Use velocity, device, and location signals to flag fraud early.
4. Early-warning credit signals: Monitor utilization spikes and payment slippage to trigger proactive outreach.
5. Unified customer view: Link deposits, cards, loans, and digital behavior for better risk and engagement decisions.
6. Spend forecasting: Use historical patterns to predict next-month expenses and alert customers early.
7. Self-service insights: Show customers simple spend and risk dashboards to encourage healthier behavior.
8. Geo-behavior monitoring: Detect unusual location changes to reduce fraud and mule activity
